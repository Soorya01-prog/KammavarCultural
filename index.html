Preserving Heritage, Empowering Community

The Kammavar Naidu community is a proud and historic Tamil-speaking people of Tamil Nadu with a rich legacy of valor and cultural heritage. Rooted in dharma and sacrifice, we descend from dynasties that have protected the land for over 700 years.

About Our Mission

A Foundation that helps Kammavar People for Spiritual, Cultural and Economic upliftment of society.

Our Heritage

We bow to our Kammakula Dhevathaa Sri Renugadevi Thalli. We are born into a dynasty that defended the nation and dharma for over 700 years, sacrificing countless lives. We gratefully remember the rulers of the Satavahana, Kakatiya, and Vijayanagara empires, and all who protected Tamil people and land, including Veera Kumara Kambanna and Thirumalai Nayakkar.

Our Mission

Kammavar Cultural Academy is a government-registered charitable trust dedicated to preserving the rich culture and traditions of the Kammavar Naidu community in Tamil Nadu. It also works for the legal protection and secure future of Kammavar children, promoting education and encouraging them to serve the nation with honesty in high positions of government.

Our Values

Kammavar Cultural Academy also works the legal protection of the kammavar people and the safe future of Kammavar children. Our foundation is conducting various programs emphasising that Kammavar children should study well, work in high positions of power in the central and state governments, and serve the people and our nation in an honest manner.
